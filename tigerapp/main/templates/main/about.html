Find your stuff!

TigerSearch is a centralized lost and found platform for Princeton students and staff to connect missing items with their owners. Using our platform is easy: post items you've lost or found around campus, search and filter through posts, contact other students, and find your stuff.

Try Our Tutorial

COS 333 Origins

TigerSearch began as a COS 333 project created by students in the semester of Fall 2021. Noticing the inefficiency and clutter of blasting listservs to locate and return lost items around campus, TigerSearch hopes to provide a simpler and faster lost and found system for Princeton's campus.
